Announcing Room View 3D

  • Maud Buffier
  • Friday, Jun 19, 2020

Today we’re excited to announce that we’re working with Valve Software (creators of Half Life, Steam, and the Index HMD).

Our work is at the intersection of computer vision and VR, and our first feature, “Room View 3D” ships as part of SteamVR 1.13 for everyone with a Valve Index headset.

Hiring in Seattle, Madrid, and remote.
